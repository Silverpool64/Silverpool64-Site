Hello! My name is Daniel (a.k.a. Silverpool64)

I'm an aspiring Electronic Music Producer hailing from Washington state. I spend my time learning the ways of FL Studio 20, hopefully making some pleasing music in the process. I love video games, especially their music. If you do as well, check out OverClocked ReMix, a community dedicated to the appreciation and promotion of video game music as an art form. They also have a bunch of FREE video game ReMixes. This is one of my favorites: "Chemical Burn" by OverShield

I practice video game ReMixing by entering music competitions like this one: PRC (Peoples Remix Competition)

You can find more competitions here.

Whenever I get the chance, I help with sound production when my friends make movies, and I'm interested in coding, as well as making websites.
